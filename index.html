Welcome to Birds mansion

The Birds mansion Hotel is a modern, elegant 4-star hotel overlooking the heart of keetmanshoop, with perfect African vibes. The rooms at the Birds mansion Hotel are new, well-lit and inviting. Our reception staff will be happy to help you during your stay. While you enjoy a cocktail by the swimming pool , you will be stunned by the breathtaking view of Keetmanshoop. During your stays, our Restaurant serves traditional Namibian dishes, snacks ,drinks and salads.
